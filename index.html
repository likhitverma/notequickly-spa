    About NoteQuickly SPA

    NoteQuickly SPA

    Version 2.0

    A fast, lightweight note-taking application for temporary notes stored locally in your browser. Perfect for quick thoughts, reminders, and drafts that you need instant access to.

    Developer

    Likhit Verma

    Features

    • Rich text formatting (bold, italic, underline, strikethrough)
    • Multiple heading styles and text alignment
    • Color customization for text and highlights
    • Ordered and unordered lists
    • Link insertion capability
    • Dark and light themes
    • Auto-save functionality
    • Download notes as text files
    • Search and filter notes
    • Fullscreen distraction-free mode
    • Word and character count

    Privacy Statement

    All notes are stored locally in your browser. No data is sent to any server. Your notes remain completely private and under your control.
